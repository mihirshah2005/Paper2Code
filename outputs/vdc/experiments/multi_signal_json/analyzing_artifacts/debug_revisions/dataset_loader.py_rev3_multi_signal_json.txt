• This function modifies the clean training data to inject dirty samples. Two types of corruptions are applied: poisoned sample injection (backdoor triggers) and noisy label injection.

  - The implementation now explicitly states that by default a sample selected for poisoning will not be considered for noisy label modification.

  - This ordering ensures no overlapping modifications occur, thereby eliminating the risk of a poisoned sample also having its label flipped via noise injection.
